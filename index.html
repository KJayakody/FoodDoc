App Features

Awesome Features

Scanning

The scanning feature in the FoodDoc app allows users to quickly and accurately identify and log food items by simply scanning their barcodes or taking pictures of the food. This feature is designed to enhance user convenience, making it easier to track nutritional intake, monitor dietary restrictions, and maintain a balanced diet.

AI ChatBot

The AI Chatbot in the FoodDoc app serves as a personalized nutrition assistant, designed to help users make informed dietary choices and achieve their health goals. Powered by advanced artificial intelligence, the chatbot offers real-time support, tailored recommendations, and interactive engagement, making healthy eating more accessible and manageable.

Community

The Community feature in the FoodDoc app is designed to create a supportive and interactive environment where users can connect, share experiences, and motivate each other on their health and nutrition journeys. This feature fosters a sense of belonging and provides valuable insights through collective knowledge and encouragement from fellow users.

Nutritional Analysis

Our nutritional analysis feature provides a detailed breakdown of the nutrients in your meals. Easily access information on macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, and fats) and micronutrients (vitamins and minerals) to understand the full nutritional profile of what you're eating.

Educational resource

The Educational Resource feature in FoodDoc is designed to empower users with comprehensive knowledge about nutrition, healthy eating habits, and food-related health conditions. Whether you’re a beginner looking to improve your diet or someone with specific dietary needs, this feature is your go-to hub for reliable and actionable information.

Collaberating with Professional

The "Collaborating with Professionals" feature in FoodDoc is designed to bridge the gap between users and industry experts, enabling seamless interaction, advice, and guidance on nutrition, diet planning, and culinary skills. Whether you're looking to improve your eating habits, need personalized diet plans, or want to enhance your cooking techniques, this feature connects you with the right professionals to help you achieve your goals.
